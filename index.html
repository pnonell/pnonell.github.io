Hi! I'm Pau and this is my portfolio.

I am an Associate Professor and Researcher at the Engineering Department of La Salle - Universitat Ramon Llull. I am a Multimedia Engineer and hold a MSc in Intelligent Interactive Systems, and also have work experience in the areas of web development and programming. I consider myself a responsible and creative person, eager to contribute new ideas and participate in their execution. From a technical point of view I am interested in the development of technologies such as Computer Vision or Artificial Intelligence, complemented with a good design and visual impact. I believe that effectively combining technology and design is a key success factor for any project.

The development of these new technologies will and is having a huge impact in society and I want to be part of that process, because I believe that it can improve people's lives in many ways.

As a professor...

... i've been principally teaching courses related to interaction design, project development, and product prototyping. In those, students had to take a specific role in a group to design and implement a technological product or an interactive installation by using their interdisciplinary skills. Some of the lessons included how to use a Raspberry, electronics, programming, 3d printing, prototyping, user testing, and organizational methods, among others. But most of the time was dedicated to putting into practice this knowledge, implementing and mentoring their projects. Their efforts and motivation led to several high-quality products, some of which are shown below.

CreactivePad: a portable trackpad connected to an app, that allows you to play music, personalize its tones and record melodies.
Mikus: a virtual pet that motivates children to follow a routine of good daily habits, with a system of gamification and animations.
Ducktory: a shopping receipt reader that keeps an inventory of the goods in the house and divides the costs among its inhabitants.
