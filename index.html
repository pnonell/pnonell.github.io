Hi! I'm Pau and this is my portfolio.

I am an Associate Professor and Researcher at the Engineering Department of La Salle - Universitat Ramon Llull. I am a Multimedia Engineer and hold a MSc in Intelligent Interactive Systems, and also have work experience in the areas of web development and programming. I consider myself a responsible and creative person, eager to contribute with new ideas and participate in their execution. From a technical point of view I am interested in the development of technologies such as Computer Vision or Artificial Intelligence, complemented with a good design and visual impact. I believe that effectively combining technology and design is a key success factor for any project.

The development of these new technologies will and is having a huge impact in society and I want to be part of that process, because I believe that it can improve people's lives in many ways.

As a professor...

    ... i've been mostly teaching courses related to interaction design, project development, and product prototyping. In these, the students had to take a specific role in a group to design and implement a technological product or an interactive installation by using their interdisciplinary skills.
    Some of the lessons included how to use a Raspberry, electronics, programming, 3d printing, prototyping, user testing, and organizational methods, among others. But most of the time was dedicated to putting into practice this knowledge, implementing and mentoring their projects. Their efforts and motivation led to several high-quality products, some of which are shown below.

CreactivePad: a portable trackpad connected to an app, that allows you to play music, personalize its tones and record melodies.
Mikus: a virtual pet that motivates children to follow a routine of good daily habits, with a system of gamification and animations.
Ducktory: a shopping receipt reader that keeps an inventory of the goods in the house and divides the costs among its inhabitants.
MetaBalance: a smart-balance that helps you to control your diet through an app linked to your mobile.
Leave with all: a system for the elder people that reminds them of the essential items before they leave home.
Dettbox: a house game that links to its resident's phones, counts the hours they spend on social networks and motivates them to spend fewer hours on the screen.
PillTech: an electronic pillbox that warns its users which medication to take at right time with lights and sounds.
iBump: an accessory for a bike with an ultrasonic sensor that warns you in case of collision.
Space Sound: interactive installation that projects information of planets and allows its users to hear how would their voice sound like in those.
Play Dio: interactive game that takes a photo of the user and allows them to change the look of their face according to some parameters.
Timeles essence: Interactive installation that aims to illustrate the trace of time by showing how the silhouettes of the users are gradually fading.
